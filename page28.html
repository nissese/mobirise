Bible Cake

Ingredients
4 1/2 cuos  1 Kings 4:22a
1 1/2 cups Proverbs 30:33
1 1/2 cups Judges 14:14
2 cups Isaiah 5:4 (dried)
2 cups Nahum 3:12
1 cup Numbers 17:8
1/2 cup 1 Corinthians 3:2
6 pieces Jeremiah 17:11
1 pinch Mark 9:50
2 teaspoons baking powder

Instructions
Take the Proverbs 30:33 and Judges 14:14 and Jeremiah 17:11 and
and do as King Solomon advises in Proverbs 23:14. Remove the seeds
of Nahum 3:12 and cut this plus the Isaiah 5:4 in small pieces. Peel and
chop Numbers 17:8. Mix well with 1 Kings 4:22 and Mark 9:50 and baking
powder. Combine with 1 Corinthians 3:2. Put the whole Romans 11:16 on a prepared bakking sheet. Put in Hosea 7:4 and cook
at 180 C for Matthew 20:12 or longer.
