Sweet Potato Biscuits

Yields: 12
Cooking Time: 17 min

Ingredients
• 1 cup sifted bread flour
• 3 teaspoons baking powder
• 3/4 teaspoon salt
• 2 tablespoons butter
• 1 cup cooked mashed sweet potatoes
• cold milk to make a soft dough (depends on moisture content of potatoes)

Instructions
1. Heat oven to 450 degrees F.
2. Combine flour, baking powder and salt and sift into mixing bowl.
3. Cut in butter, rubbing it in with tips of fingers.
4. Add sweet potatoes, then 2 to 4 tablespoons of milk. Mix.
